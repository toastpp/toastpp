toastMapSolToMesh

Map an image between two different basis representations: from s-basis to h-basis (see Basis definitions).

Syntax

himg = toastMapSolToMesh (hBasis, simg)

Parameters

[In] hBasishandlebasis mapper handle
simgreal or complex [s]image (basis coefficients) in s-basis expansion
[Out] himgreal or complex [h]image (basis coefficients) mapped into h-basis expansion
Dimensions: s = size of s-basis, h = size of h-basis.

Notes

Arbitrary functions can generally not be represented exactly in any of the basis expansions. Therefore, mapping from one basis to another may incur a loss, and mapping back to the first basis may not recover the original basis coefficients precisely.

Images can be real or complex-valued.
