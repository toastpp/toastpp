toastMapMeshToBasis

Map an image between two different basis representations: from h-basis to b-basis (see Basis definitions).

Syntax

bimg = toastMapBasisToMesh (hBasis, himg)

Parameters

[In] hBasishandlebasis mapper handle
himgreal or complex [h]image (basis coefficients) in (nodal) h-basis expansion
[Out] bimgreal or complex [b]image (basis coefficients) mapped into (regular) b-basis expansion
Dimensions: h = size of h-basis, b = size of b-basis.

Notes

Arbitrary functions can generally not be represented exactly in any of the basis expansions. Therefore, mapping from one basis to another may incur a loss, and mapping back to the first basis may not recover the original basis coefficients precisely.

Images can be real or complex-valued.

If the hBasis handle was created by a call to toastSetBasis with only a single regular grid parameter, then toastMapMeshToBasis is identical to toastMapMeshToGrid.
