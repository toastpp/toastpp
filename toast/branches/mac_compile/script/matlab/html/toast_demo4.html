TOAST Demo 4: Source-detector arrangements

The number of boundary sources and detectors, and their arrangement on the surface of the object, has a significant influence on the performance of the reconstruction.

This demo shows the effect of various detector patterns on a reconstruction problem in a circular domain.

Select one of the patterns from the list. You can see the arrangement of detectors used by the first source in the image on the top right. The relative position of detectors is the same for all sources.

Click 'Run' to start the reconstruction.

The full sampling patterns provide the best reconstruction results, followed by the fanbeam transmission patterns. The reflection patterns give the poorest results, with little sensitivity in the centre of the object.
